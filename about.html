About me

I'm passionate about creative and immersive content, particularly in the world of website design. As a lifelong creator, I have a natural flair for problem-solving and an endless drive to understand the world. In autumn of 2023 I enrolled at the Multimedia designer education at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology, hoping to indulge in a creative surrounding - which I indeed did. But little did I know I would also fall in love with something new, developing websites.

I believe this education has introduced me to a niche where I'm very excited to grow and become a part of. I am eager to further cultivate my skills in creative development and contribute to creating fun and interactive experiences.

I'm half-Swedish and half-British, and I moved to Copenhagen, Denmark in 2023. My move to Copenhagen has definitely managed to broaden my creative vision, with the way of life and the people in this city.

Merits

I am a skilled illustrator, who are familiar with Adobe Creative Suite, Procreate and Figma.

In the realm of web development, am I rapidly evolving my skills in HTML, CSS and some vanilla JavaScript.

I believe a lot in constant learning, and I do get out of my way to make sure I understand as much about my tasks as possible, through experimenting, such as trying to find a new solution to a problem, and by studying and reading up on the literature behind something.

During my multiple year-long experience at a highly automated operating warehouse in Sweden, it became clear to me that one of my main strengths is tackling challenges heads-on and problem solving.
